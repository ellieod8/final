Delivery

SolarSphere360 ensures the safe and timely delivery of your products, whether it’s a Solar Sphere, Abstract Sphere, or a Big Service installation kit. Products are delivered within 7-10 business days for local customers. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order size. Need it sooner? Please note: Delivery timelines may vary based on location and product availability.

Return Policies

At SolarSphere360, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction. However, if you’re not fully satisfied with your purchase, we offer a hassle-free return policy. Customers can return products within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange. Items must be returned in their original condition, including all packaging, manuals, and accessories. Products installed by The Big Service are only eligible for return if installation defects are reported within the first 14 days. Custom-sized or specially modified Solar Sphere products are non-returnable unless damaged during shipping. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the returned item. For more details or exceptions, please refer to our complete Return Policy documentation.
